About Us :




Welcome to Kanha Tour and Travels, your premier travel partner operating in the picturesque state of Himachal Pradesh. We are dedicated to providing exceptional travel experiences, offering a wide range of services to cater to all your transportation and travel needs. Whether you are planning a serene getaway in the mountains, a thrilling adventure through the valleys, or a comprehensive exploration of Himachal's rich cultural heritage, we have you covered.


At Kanha Tour and Travels, we offer the convenience of vehicle reservations, ensuring that you have access to transportation exactly when you need it. Our extensive fleet includes economy cars for budget travelers, luxurious options for special occasions, spacious SUVs for family trips, and vans and trucks for larger groups or equipment transportation. No matter your preference, we have the perfect vehicle to suit your journey.


For those planning extended stays, our long-term rental options provide flexible and cost-effective solutions, allowing you to rent vehicles on a weekly, monthly, or extended basis. We also offer one-way rentals, giving you the freedom to pick up a vehicle at one location and drop it off at another, perfect for road trips, business travel, or relocation needs.


Kanha Tour and Travels is committed to delivering personalized service and ensuring that your travel experience in Himachal Pradesh is both memorable and hassle-free. Explore the beauty and charm of this enchanting region with us, and let us take care of all your travel needs.

Our Services :

Vehicle Reservations and Variety:

Kanha Tour and Travels offers the convenience of reserving vehicles for specific dates and times, ensuring that customers have access to transportation when needed. They provide a wide range of vehicles to choose from, including economy cars for budget-conscious travelers, luxury cars for special occasions, SUVs for family trips, and vans and trucks for larger groups or transporting equipment. This variety ensures that customers can find a vehicle that suits their specific requirements and preferences.

Long-term Rentals:


For those who require vehicles for extended periods, Kanha Tour and Travels offers flexible long-term rental options. Customers can rent vehicles on a weekly, monthly, or extended basis, making it convenient for long vacations, business trips, or temporary transportation needs. These long-term rental plans are designed to provide cost-effective solutions and greater convenience for customers who need a vehicle for a longer duration.


One-way Rentals:


Kanha Tour and Travels provides the flexibility of one-way rentals, allowing customers to pick up a vehicle at one location and drop it off at another. This service is ideal for road trips, business travel, or relocating, as it eliminates the need to return the vehicle to the original pick-up location. This flexibility enhances the convenience of travel, making it easier for customers to plan their journeys according to their specific needs and schedules.
